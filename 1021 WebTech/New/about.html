RockSoc FAQ's

  • How much does a membership cost, and how long does it last for?

    A membership to RockSoc costs £5, regardless of whether you're a student of the University of Leeds, and it lasts the entire academic year.

  • What happens in RockSoc Friday socials?

    Friday is our night out! We usually head to the Dry Dock pub at around 8pm to let our members socialise before heading out to The Key Club for as long as you're willing to stay!

  • What other benefits do I get with my membership?

    Our Treasurer crunched the numbers to get a deal with the Dry Dock pub to sell cocktails to members off the bar for only £3! Additionally, you get a cheaper entry to The Key Club on Fridays, costing £4. Just show your card to them and they'll be happy to accept it.

  • I've bought a membership, how do I get my card and member status online?

    Speak to our treasurer about getting a card, and they'll be able to provide you with one. Speak with the President on Discord and they'll validate your membership role for you.

  • What other events do you have planned in the future?

    Keep checking the Schedule to stay up to date!
